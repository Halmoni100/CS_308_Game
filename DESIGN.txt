Genre: Shooting & Aiming game
Name: Pay Attention Class

Goal:

You are a teacher of a high school teacher class, but many of your students are !  Your objective is to fire pencils from your desk to any sleeping student to wake them up before they all fall asleep.

Basic mechanics:

Throw pencils by adjusting an indicated arrow to aim at the students and space to fire.

Levels:

Basic level only has students that fall asleep.  Advanced level also has moving troublemaker that must be defused within a certain time frame.
