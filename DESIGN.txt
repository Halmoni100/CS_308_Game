Genre: Shooting & Aiming game
Name: Pay Attention Workers

Goal:

You are a manager of a company, but many of your workers are sleepy!  Your objective is to fire office scrap to any sleeping worker to wake them up before they all fall asleep.

Basic mechanics:

Throw office scrap by adjusting an indicated arrow to aim at the students and space to fire.  Move around manager using WASD keys.

Levels:

Basic mode has manager moving at constant velocity.  Advanced level has manager moving with acceleration.
